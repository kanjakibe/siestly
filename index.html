Questions About Siestly?

We're here to help. Check out our FAQ.

Do I need to buy a separate pillow case?

The outer cover of our Siestly bed pillows is machine washable and dryer friendly, so there is no need to purchase an additional pillow cover. However, if you are interested in personalizing your pillow, you can explore our selection of pillow covers and other pillow accessories.

What is the difference between shredded memory foam and cross-cut memory foam?

Shredded memory foam pillows are made of tiny, shredded pieces of memory foam filling, allowing for more breathability due to additional air circulation. At Siestly, we prefer cross-cut foam for its consistency, non-clumping, and improved air circulation compared to traditional shredded memory foam.

Is cross-cut microfiber good for back and neck pain?

Our Siestly bed pillows, filled with cross-cut microfiber and 100% virgin memory foam, are designed to be adjustable and provide pressure relief. This adjustability allows for proper spinal alignment, supporting back and neck pain relief for all sleeping positions.

Do memory foam pillows go flat?

If your Siestly bed pillow begins to feel flat, we recommend putting it in the dryer for 10 minutes to fluff up the 100% virgin memory foam fill.

How do I clean my Siestly pillow?

You can easily clean your Siestly pillow by removing the outer cover and machine washing it. For the inner pillow, spot cleaning is recommended as needed. Avoid soaking the memory foam core.

Can I adjust the firmness of my Siestly pillow?

Yes, our Siestly pillows are designed with adjustable filling. You can add or remove the cross-cut memory foam to tailor the pillows height and firmness to your comfort preferences.

Are Siestly pillows hypoallergenic?

Absolutely! Our Siestly pillows are made with hypoallergenic materials, ensuring they are suitable for sleepers with allergies or sensitivities.

Will the Siestly pillow support side sleepers?

Yes, the Siestly pillow is versatile enough to support side sleepers, back sleepers, and stomach sleepers alike. Its adjustable fill allows you to customize the pillow to your preferred sleeping position.

Is there a break-in period for the Siestly pillow?

Some sleepers might experience a break-in period as the memory foam adjusts to your body contours. We recommend giving the pillow a few days to fully expand and adjust to your sleeping style.

What is the warranty on the Siestly pillow?

We stand behind the quality of our Siestly pillows with a comprehensive one-year warranty that covers any defects in materials and workmanship.

How often should I replace my Siestly pillow?

For optimal comfort and hygiene, we recommend replacing your Siestly pillow every 18 to 24 months, depending on personal usage and care.

Can the Siestly pillow help reduce snoring?

Many of our customers have reported that the adjustable nature of our Siestly pillow has helped them maintain better alignment of their airways, which can contribute to reducing snoring.

Is the Siestly pillow suitable for kids?

Yes, the Siestly pillow is safe for children. Its adjustable fill allows you to customize the pillow to suit the comfort and support needs of your child.

How does the Siestly pillow stay cool throughout the night?

The Siestly pillow is designed with breathable materials and a ventilated memory foam core that promotes airflow, helping to keep the pillow cool throughout the night.

Can I travel with my Siestly pillow?

Absolutely! The Siestly pillow is lightweight and can be compressed to fit in your travel bag, ensuring you get a great night's sleep wherever you go.
